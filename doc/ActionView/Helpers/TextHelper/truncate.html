ActionView::Helpers::TextHelper#truncate

Truncates a given +text+ after a given :length if +text+ is longer than :length (defaults to 30). The last characters will be replaced with the :omission (defaults to "...") for a total length not exceeding :length. Pass a :separator to truncate +text+ at a natural break. The result is not marked as HTML-safe, so will be subject to the default escaping when used in views, unless wrapped by raw().
