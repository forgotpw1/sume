Date#to_time

Converts a Date instance to a Time, where the time is set to the beginning of the day. The timezone can be either :local or :utc (default :local). date = Date.new(2007, 11, 10) # => Sat, 10 Nov 2007 date.to_time # => Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 0800 2007 date.to_time(:local) # => Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 0800 2007 date.to_time(:utc) # => Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 UTC 2007
