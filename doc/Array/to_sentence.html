Array#to_sentence

Converts the array to a comma-separated sentence where the last element is joined by the connector word. Options: * :words_connector - The sign or word used to join the elements in arrays with two or more elements (default: ", ") * :two_words_connector - The sign or word used to join the elements in arrays with two elements (default: " and ") * :last_word_connector - The sign or word used to join the last element in arrays with three or more elements (default: ", and ")
